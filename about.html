About Us

Introducing Power EV, a software designed to cater to all your electric vehicle charging requirements.

Problem Statement

There is a growing number of electric vehicle (EV) owners who need access to charging stations to keep their vehicles charged.However, the availability of charging stations and the lack of real-time information on their occupancy can lead to long waiting times and inefficient use of resources.This creates a need for an app that can allow EV owners to easily locate and reserve available charging slots, reducing wait times and improving the overall charging experience. By solving these issues, the app can make the charging process more convenient and efficient, encouraging more people to switch to electric vehicles and reducing the overall environmental impact of transportation.

Our Solution

The application is designed to furnish users with pertinent details regarding charging station locations, availability of connectors, and associated pricing. Additionally, users can utilize the app to make payments for their charging sessions, and we also offer navigation assistance to guide them to the designated charging station.

United Nations SDGs we are Addressing

  • Affordable and Clean Energy: Our goal is to simplify the process of charging electric vehicles, with the aim of promoting greater use of these vehicles
  • Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure: Moreover we want to address the lack of infrastructure in the ev charging technology and we want to make the infrastructue more organised.
  • Climate Action: We also want to decrease the carbon footprint of the transportation industry by promoting the adoption of clean energy, specifically electricity thereby battling against climate change.
